our trips

DIYAR TRAVELS Trips

Blue mosque

The Al Farooq Omar Bin Al Khattab Mosque, also known as the Blue Mosque, modeled after Istanbul's iconic mosque.

Safari & Arabic Dinner

A thrilling desert adventure combining exhilarating dune bashing, traditional Bedouin hospitality, and authentic Arabic cuisine under the stars, providing a unique cultural experience amidst the mesmerizing desert landscape.

Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque

In Abu Dhabi is a majestic architectural marvel, renowned for its 82 domes, 24-carat gold chandeliers, and the world's largest hand-knotted carpet.

Louvre Museum

A pinnacle of art and culture, housing iconic masterpieces, spanning centuries and civilizations, showcasing the essence of human creativity and heritage in the heart of Paris.

Old Dubai

It's a historic district rich in cultural heritage, with traditional markets, museums, and historic sites along Dubai Creek.

Burj Al Arab

Iconic sail-shaped hotel, renowned for its luxury, opulence, and breathtaking views of the Arabian Gulf, epitomizing Dubai's extravagance and hospitality.

Burjkhalifa

The tallest skyscraper globally, an architectural wonder, and a symbol of Dubai's ambition and innovation.

Qasr Al-Watan

the Palace of the Nation, offers a captivating journey into Emirati heritage and governance. With stunning architecture and immersive exhibitions, it provides unparalleled insight into the UAE's history and culture.

We aspire to lead as the premier global provider of guided tours, distinguished for our dedication to authenticity, sustainability, and unmatched customer satisfaction

DIYAR TRAVELS Vission

Diyar Travels' Vission

our values

DIYAR TRAVELS Values

01 Authenticity

Diyar Travels upholds authenticity by providing authentic experiences that accurately reflect local culture, traditions, and way of life, fostering genuine interactions with local communities.

02 Sustainability

Diyar Travels is dedicated to sustainability, promoting responsible travel practices that preserve natural resources and positively impact the communities they visit.

03 Customer Satisfaction

Diyar Travels focuses on delivering exceptional service, personalized experiences, and exceeding expectations through feedback, responsiveness, and continuous improvement, ensuring a memorable and fulfilling journey.
